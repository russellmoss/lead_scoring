## ðŸ“ Methodology

### Data Source
- **Table**: `savvy-gtm-analytics.FinTrx_data_CA.ria_contacts_current`
- **Filters Applied**:
  - Must have `PRIMARY_FIRM_START_DATE` (current employment)
  - Must have `PRIMARY_FIRM_NAME` and `PRIMARY_FIRM` (firm information)
  - Excludes firms with >100% turnover (collapsed firms)
  - Excludes firms with <20 reps (minimum viable firm size)

### Tier Logic Applied
- **V3.2 tier scoring logic** applied to all contacts
- Same logic as lead list query (Section K from `January_2026_Lead_List_Query_V3.2.sql`)
- Includes certification detection (CFP, Series 65)
- Includes firm metrics (bleeding, size, turnover)
- Includes advisor history (mobility, tenure, experience)

### Exclusions
- **Wirehouses**: Excluded from priority tiers (but counted in population)
- **Collapsed Firms**: Firms with >100% turnover excluded
- **Small Firms**: Firms with <20 reps excluded (minimum viable)
